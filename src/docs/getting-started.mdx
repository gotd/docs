---
title: 'Getting started'
description: Learn how to get started with gotd.
---

First, you need to obtain `api_id` and `api_hash` from "App configuration" on https://my.telegram.org/apps.

Login to telegram and create new application if needed.

**Never share or hardcode `api_id` or `api_hash`!***


Once you have obtained `app_id` and `app_hash` you can try [starting echo bot](/example/echo-bot).
